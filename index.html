FIONA QIU

Fiona Qiu is currently completing her Master of Arts at Mozarteum University under the tutelage of Professor Lily Francis. While training at the Australian National Academy of Music (ANAM), she was mentored by Dr. Robin Wilson and Sophie Rowell at ANAM, as well as Michele Walsh at the Queensland Conservatorium Griffith University (QCGU).

Fiona has won numerous first-prize awards and scholarships, including the Ian Potter Cultural Trust Award, the QCGU Ena Williams Award, the Postgraduate Prize, the Brisbane Clubs Award & Audience Choice Award, the Owen Fletcher Prize, the Barbara Hebden Bursary, the Matilda Jane Aplin Prize, the Vada Jefferies Prize, the Basil Jones Sonata Prize, the Nora Baird Bursary, the German Violin Travel Bursary, and the Griffith Award for Academic Excellence.
