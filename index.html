FIONA QIU

The Sleepless Violin

Fiona Qiu completed a Bachelor of Music at Queensland Conservatorium Griffith University under Michele Walsh. She won several prestigious awards, including the Ena Williams Award, Postgraduate Prize, and Griffith Award for Academic Excellence. In 2018, she performed in the Musica Viva masterclass series with renowned violinist Ray Chen.

As a soloist, Fiona was featured on 4MBS Classic FM as the winner of the QCGU Brisbane Clubs Award and the Club’s Audience Choice Award. She was a soloist with the Queensland Conservatorium Symphony Orchestra and the Kelvin Grove Orchestra.

Fiona served as Concertmaster for the Queensland Conservatorium Symphony Orchestra's performance of Strauss’ Ein Heldenleben, tackling one of the most challenging solos in the repertoire. She was twice Concertmaster at the Australian Youth Orchestra’s National Music Camp and participated in the 2019 AYO International Tour and Chamber Players programs.
